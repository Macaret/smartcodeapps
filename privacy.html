Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Smart Code Apps is committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our apps do not collect personal information from children, in compliance with COPPA and GDPR-K.

1. Who we are

Smart Code Apps ("we", "our", or "us") is an independent software studio based in Romania. We develop mobile and web applications. You can contact us at lovin.smartcodeapps@gmail.com.

2. Apps covered by this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published by Smart Code Apps, including:

  • Învață să Citești (Learn to Read) — an educational literacy app for children ages 5–7

Each app may also have its own supplementary privacy notice where applicable.

3. Information we do not collect

Our apps are designed with privacy by default. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Contacts or device identifiers linked to a person
  • Financial or payment information

4. Technical data we may collect

To improve app performance and stability, we may collect limited, anonymous technical data:

  • Crash reports: Anonymous technical logs to help us fix bugs (via Firebase Crashlytics). These contain no personal data.
  • Usage analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data about app features used (via Firebase Analytics without advertising identifiers). This data cannot be used to identify any individual user.

All analytics are configured without advertising IDs and without any personal identifiers, in full compliance with Apple's requirements for children's apps.

5. Voice recognition

Our Învață să Citești app uses voice recognition to help children practice pronunciation. All voice processing occurs entirely on-device using Apple's built-in speech recognition framework. No voice recordings are transmitted to our servers or any third party.

6. No advertising

Our apps contain no third-party advertising. We do not display ads and do not share any data with advertising networks.

7. In-app purchases

Any in-app purchases are processed exclusively through Apple's App Store. We do not handle or store payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions.

8. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services in our apps:

  • Firebase (Google): Crash reporting and anonymous analytics, configured without advertising identifiers. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple frameworks: On-device speech recognition, App Store payments. Apple Privacy Policy

9. Parental rights

We do not collect personal information from children or their parents, so there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, contact us at lovin.smartcodeapps@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

10. Data retention

Crash reports and aggregated analytics data are retained for up to 12 months, after which they are automatically deleted. Since this data is entirely anonymous and contains no personal identifiers, it cannot be linked to any individual user.

11. Children's privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Our apps are directed at children under 13. We comply with:

  • The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • The General Data Protection Regulation as applied to children (GDPR-K)
  • Apple's Kids Category guidelines and App Store Review Guidelines

We do not collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures appropriate to the nature of data we handle. Since we collect only anonymous, non-personal technical data, the privacy risk to users is minimal. All communication between our apps and any services uses encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage parents to review this policy periodically.

14. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: lovin.smartcodeapps@gmail.com
  • Company: Smart Code Apps
  • Country: Romania
